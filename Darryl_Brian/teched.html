Tech Talk at SBHS

This Google Site, which is created and maintained be and the other SBHS Tech Educator, is used at SBHS for many purposes. This includes making a reservation for a Chromecart - a cart of 30 Chromebooks to be used in a classroom - and making a reservation for the Digital Learning Center - a larger learning space for teachers to bring their classes and use a chromebox that connects to a projector. Their are also tutorials and news, many of which are written by me, that include screenshots and videos of all the technology we want our staff to use which includes Google Drive, Chromebooks, and many other educational and productivity web tools.

Mr. Mendonez's Class Wiki

This is my class wiki which I use daily for all of my classes. Here students, parents, teachers, and administrators can find my daily agenda to see what I a covering each day in class. Students access it as soon as they come in to class and are able to get started with their work without being told. All resources students need can also be accessed here which inludes links to articles, videos, Google Documents for worksheets, Google Forms for quizzes and tests, and even the website I designed to be my ebook for my web design classes.

Learn Web Design

This is the "ebook" I use in both my Introduction to Web Design and Web Animation courses.
